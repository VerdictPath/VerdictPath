## üìã Phase 4: Cross-Portal Collaboration Testing

**Goal:** Test how the three portals work together in coordinated scenarios

### Testing Groups
You'll be assigned to a small group with:
- 1 Individual tester
- 1 Law Firm tester
- 1 Medical Provider tester

### Collaborative Test Scenarios

#### Scenario 1: New Case Workflow
1. **Individual:** Create a new injury case and send it to the law firm
2. **Law Firm:** Accept the case and review details
3. **Law Firm:** Request medical records from the provider
4. **Provider:** Respond to the request and upload records
5. **All:** Verify everyone sees the updates in real-time

#### Scenario 2: Document Sharing
1. **Individual:** Upload injury photos and incident report
2. **Law Firm:** Access documents and add legal notes
3. **Law Firm:** Request additional medical documentation
4. **Provider:** Upload requested documents
5. **All:** Verify proper document permissions and access

#### Scenario 3: Communication Flow
1. **Individual:** Send a message asking about case progress
2. **Law Firm:** Respond and request medical update
3. **Provider:** Provide medical status update
4. **All:** Check notification system works across portals

#### Scenario 4: Case Updates
1. **Law Firm:** Update case status (e.g., "In Negotiation")
2. **Provider:** Add treatment notes
3. **Individual:** View updates on dashboard
4. **All:** Verify timeline and activity feed accuracy

### What to Look For
- ‚ö° **Speed:** Are updates reflected quickly?
- üîí **Security:** Can you only see what you should see?
- üì± **Notifications:** Are you notified of relevant actions?
- üéØ **Accuracy:** Is all information correct and synchronized?

## üìã Phase 5: Real-World Simulation (Anonymous Testing)

**Goal:** Test the app as it would be used in the real world, without knowing who your collaborators are

### How It Works
- You'll be assigned a testing partner (or partners)
- You will **NOT** know who they are
- You'll only interact through the VerdictPath platform
- This simulates real-world usage where users don't know each other beforehand

### Your Assignment
**You will receive:**
- Your portal role (Individual, Law Firm, or Provider)
- A case scenario to work through
- Specific tasks to complete
- No information about who you're working with

### Example Scenarios

#### If you're assigned as an Individual:
- Create a case for a car accident injury
- Connect with an assigned law firm (you'll receive their firm name)
- Request records from an assigned provider
- Communicate only through the platform

#### If you're assigned as a Law Firm:
- Accept an incoming case from an unknown individual
- Request medical records from an assigned provider
- Manage the case using only the information in the platform

#### If you're assigned as a Medical Provider:
- Respond to record requests
- Provide medical documentation
- Communicate only through secure platform channels

### What We're Testing
- üîê **Privacy:** Does the platform maintain appropriate boundaries?
- üí¨ **Communication:** Can users collaborate effectively without outside contact?
- üé≠ **User Experience:** Does the platform provide enough context?
- üöÄ **Workflow:** Can a case be managed entirely within the platform?

Delete Your Verdict Path Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Verdict Path account, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App Deletion

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the Verdict Path app
  2. Go to Profile or Settings
  3. Scroll down to Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision
Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to request account deletion:

Email: support@verdictpath.com

Please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your litigation progress and case data
  • Your uploaded documents
  • Your message history
  • Your notification preferences
  • Your payment history (Stripe records may be retained per their policy)
  • Your achievements and gamification data
Important Notice:
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Deletion will be processed within 30 days of your request
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (audit logs, HIPAA requirements)
  • If you have an active subscription, please cancel it before deleting your account

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at support@verdictpath.com

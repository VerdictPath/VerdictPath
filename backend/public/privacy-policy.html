Privacy Policy for Verdict Path

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Verdict Path ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

Verdict Path is a civil litigation case management application designed to help individual users, law firms, and medical providers manage case progress, communications, and related activities.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • First and last name
  • User type (individual user, law firm, or medical provider)
  • Firm name or provider name (if applicable)
  • Subscription tier selection

Profile Information:

  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Timezone information

Case and Legal Information:

  • Litigation stage progress
  • Case-related notes and information you provide
  • Task completion data
  • Calendar event details
  • Connection information (law firm and medical provider relationships)

Payment Information: We use Stripe, a third-party payment processor, to process payments. We do not store your complete credit card information on our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address

Usage Information:

  • App features accessed
  • Time spent in the app
  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • Login frequency and patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process your transactions and subscriptions
  • Track litigation progress through the 9-stage roadmap
  • Enable communication between users, law firms, and medical providers
  • Deliver push notifications and alerts
  • Synchronize calendar events
  • Improve and personalize the Service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information:

  • With other users you connect with (law firms, medical providers)
  • With service providers (Stripe for payments, Railway for hosting, Expo for notifications)
  • For legal reasons when required by law
  • In connection with business transfers
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data
  • HTTPS/TLS for data in transit
  • Secure password hashing
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits

6. HIPAA Compliance

For medical providers and related health information, Verdict Path implements HIPAA-compliant security measures including encryption, access controls, audit logging, and secure document storage.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control notification preferences

8. Children's Privacy

Verdict Path is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

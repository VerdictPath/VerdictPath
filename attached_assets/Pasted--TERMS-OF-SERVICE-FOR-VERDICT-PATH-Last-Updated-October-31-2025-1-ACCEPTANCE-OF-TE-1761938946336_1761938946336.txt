## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

Verdict Path is a mobile application designed to help users manage civil litigation cases. The Service provides:

**For Individual Users:**
- Interactive 9-stage litigation roadmap with 60 substages
- Case progress tracking
- Task management assigned by law firms
- Calendar integration for court dates and appointments
- Push notifications and alerts
- Connection to one law firm and multiple medical providers
- Gamification features (achievements, badges, coin system)
- Educational video library
- Notification preferences and quiet hours

**For Law Firms:**
- Client management portal
- Ability to update client litigation progress
- Push notification sending to clients
- Task assignment to clients
- Calendar event requests from clients
- Subscription management
- Client search and analytics

**For Medical Providers:**
- Patient management portal
- Appointment scheduling and event requests
- Push notification sending to patients
- Calendar integration
- Patient connection management
- Subscription management

**Payment Processing:**
- Subscription plans (Free, Basic, Premium)
- Secure payment processing through Stripe
- Multiple payment methods (credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
